Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our accounting and tax services

What accounting services do you provide?

We offer comprehensive accounting services including monthly/yearly bookkeeping, financial statement preparation, VAT services, corporate tax filing, and audit report services.

How often should I file VAT returns?

VAT returns must be filed quarterly in the UAE. We provide complete assistance with VAT filing, ensuring timely and accurate submissions.

What is GoAML registration?

GoAML is the UAE's anti-money laundering reporting platform. We assist businesses with registration, compliance, and reporting requirements.

Do you handle corporate tax filing?

Yes, we provide complete corporate tax services including annual filing (SBR/NIL), tax planning, and compliance with UAE tax regulations.

What documents do I need for VAT registration?

For VAT registration, you'll need your trade license, bank statements, financial records, and other supporting documents. We'll guide you through the complete process.

How can I claim VAT refund?

We assist with the complete VAT refund process, including documentation preparation, application submission, and follow-up with authorities.
